About Us

Motivation

Children bring joy, happiness, and new unrivaled potential to this world, but they also require 24-hour care and supervision and a lot of responsibility from any individual.

Challenge

Parents across Georgia Tech rely on childcare facilities so they can work and provide for their family. The pandemic has, however, intensified issues within the childcare sector, making it difficult to find childcare. Facilities have been closed and reduced in size during the pandemic, exacerbating already long waitlists for childcare facilities.

Approach

Our goal is to provide parents with access to emergency caregivers whenever and wherever is needed by employing Georgia Tech students to provide on-demand care and tutoring for children of Georgia Tech parents.
